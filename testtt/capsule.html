I am a Tranquilizer,
Edible situations

I’m effective at home. I work in the office. I can take exams. on the witness stand. I mend broken cups with care.

Action and Indications

I know how to handle misfortune,  how to take bad news. I can minimize injustice, lighten up God’s absence,  or pick the widow’s veil that suits your face.

Edible methods

All you have to do is take me,  let me melt beneath your tongue, just gulp me with a glass of water.

Notice

You’re still a young man/woman. It’s not too late  to learn how to unwind. Who said  you have to take it on the chin?

Directions

Let me have your abyss.  I’ll cushion it with sleep. You’ll thank me for giving you four paws to fall on.

WARNING！
